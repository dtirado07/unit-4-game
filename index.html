Crystal Collector!

Welcome! At the start of each game you will receive a random number.

By clicking on the crystals below you will add specific amount of points to your total score.

You will win if your total score is exactly the same as the random computer number if you go above that number you will lose.

Each time you play the random number will be different as well as the value for the crystals. Good luck!
